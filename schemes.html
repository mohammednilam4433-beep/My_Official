Discover available government programs and financial assistance for rural development in Sri Lanka

Available Schemes

Agriculture

Fertilizer Subsidy Program

Financial assistance for farmers to purchase fertilizers at subsidized rates.

Up to 50% Subsidy
30 Days Processing
All Farmers Eligibility
Housing

Rural Housing Grant

Financial support for low-income families to build or renovate their homes.

LKR 300,000 Maximum Grant
60 Days Processing
Low Income Eligibility
Education

Higher Education Scholarship

Scholarships for talented students from rural areas to pursue higher education.

Full Tuition Covered
45 Days Processing
A+ Students Eligibility
Health

Universal Health Insurance

Health insurance coverage for low-income families in rural areas.

90% Coverage
15 Days Processing
All Citizens Eligibility

Small Business Grant

Financial support for entrepreneurs to start or expand small businesses in rural areas.

LKR 500,000 Maximum Grant
60 Days Processing
Entrepreneurs Eligibility

Crop Insurance Scheme

100% Coverage
30 Days Processing
