Home Remedies for Common Ailments

Headache

  • Apply ginger paste to the forehead.
  • Drink plenty of water to hydrate.
  • Steam inhalation with lime leaves.
  • Rest in a dark, quiet room.

Fever

  • Drink coriander (Koththamalli) water.
  • Wipe body with lukewarm water.
  • Drink King Coconut water for hydration.
  • Avoid heavy meals.

Gastritis

  • Drink fresh coconut water.
  • Eat small meals frequently.
  • Avoid spicy and oily foods.
  • Drink cold milk to soothe burning.

Toothache

  • Chew on a clove (Karabu Nati).
  • Rinse mouth with warm salt water.
  • Apply garlic paste to the affected tooth.

Sore Throat

  • Gargle with warm salt water.
  • Drink tea with ginger and honey.
  • Chew Tulsi (Basil) leaves.

Minor Cuts

  • Clean with clean water and soap.
  • Apply turmeric powder (antiseptic).
  • Keep the wound dry and covered.

Government Health Schemes

Suwaseriya (1990)

Free pre-hospital care ambulance service available across Sri Lanka. Call 1990 toll-free from any network.

Thriposha Program

Supplementary food program for pregnant/lactating mothers and under-nourished children. Available at local MOH offices.

CKDu Support

Financial allowance and specialized clinics for kidney patients in rural agricultural districts.

Cancer Support

Financial assistance for low-income cancer patients through the Cancer Control Programme. Contact Apeksha Hospital.

First Aid Visual Guides

CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation)

1
Check Responsiveness: Shake shoulders and ask "Are you okay?". If no response, call 1990.
2
Push Hard & Fast: Place hands on center of chest. Push down 2 inches at 100-120 beats per minute.
3
Continue: Do not stop until help arrives or the person starts breathing.

Choking (Heimlich Maneuver)

1
Stand behind the person. Wrap arms around waist. Make a fist above the navel.
2
Pull inward and upward sharply. Repeat thrusts until object is expelled.

Health Awareness

Dengue Prevention

Clean surroundings to stop mosquito breeding. Cover water containers. Seek medical help for high fever.

Diabetes Control

Reduce sugar and rice intake. Exercise daily for 30 minutes. Regular blood sugar testing at rural clinics.

Mental Health

Depression is treatable. Talk to family or visit the nearest hospital's mental health unit (Mithuru Piyasa).
