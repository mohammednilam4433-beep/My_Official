Home Remedies

Headache Remedies

1. Apply a cold or warm compress to your forehead and neck

2. Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated

3. Practice relaxation techniques like deep breathing

4. Gently massage your temples and neck

5. Rest in a quiet, dark room

Fever Remedies

1. Stay hydrated with water, herbal teas, and broth

2. Take a lukewarm bath or use cool compresses

3. Rest and allow your body to recover

4. Wear lightweight clothing

5. Use a fan to keep air circulating

Cough & Cold Remedies

1. Drink warm honey and lemon water

2. Inhale steam with a few drops of eucalyptus oil

3. Gargle with warm salt water

4. Drink herbal teas with ginger and turmeric

5. Use a humidifier in your room

Minor Burns

1. Cool the burn under running water for 10-15 minutes

2. Apply aloe vera gel or honey

3. Cover with a sterile bandage

4. Avoid breaking any blisters

5. Take over-the-counter pain relievers if needed

Free delivery, C-section, drugs, diagnostics, diet, and transport for pregnant women and sick newborns.

Eligibility: All pregnant women

Benefits: Completely free delivery care

Child health screening and early intervention services for defects, diseases, deficiencies, and developmental delays.

Eligibility: Children aged 0-18 years

Benefits: Free screening and early intervention
