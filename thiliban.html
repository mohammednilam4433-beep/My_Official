Welcome to Social Welfare & Others

Social welfare is the collective effort of society to support the well-being of individuals, families, and communities. It is based on values of compassion, equality, and human dignity, ensuring that no one is left behind regardless of age, gender, or social status.

"Empowering Communities, Enriching Lives."

Our Services

Discover our comprehensive range of social welfare services designed to support and empower communities

"From Care to Empowerment."

Child Welfare Services

Programs that protect vulnerable children, support education, provide foster care, and ensure children's rights and well-being

"Every Child Deserves a Safe and Bright Future."

Child Welfare Services

Our Child Welfare Services are designed to protect and nurture children who are vulnerable, at risk, or in need of special care. We provide comprehensive support to ensure every child has a safe, healthy, and nurturing environment to grow and thrive.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Children under 18 years of age
  • Families facing financial hardship
  • Children in need of foster care or adoption
  • Children experiencing abuse or neglect
  • Children with special educational needs

Ready to Apply?

Elderly Care Services

Services that include senior homes, medical assistance, companionship, and financial support to promote dignity and independence in old age

"Caring Hands for Golden Years."

Elderly Care Services

We provide comprehensive care and support for senior citizens to ensure they live with dignity, comfort, and independence. Our programs include residential care facilities, day care centers, medical support, and social engagement activities.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Seniors aged 60 years and above
  • Individuals with limited family support
  • Low-income elderly individuals
  • Seniors requiring medical assistance
  • Individuals needing social companionship

Ready to Apply?

Healthcare & Medical Aid

Free or subsidized healthcare programs, mobile clinics, vaccination drives, and medical emergency support for marginalized communities

"Health is a Right, Not a Privilege."

Healthcare & Medical Aid

Our healthcare programs provide essential medical services to underserved communities, including free health checkups, mobile medical units, vaccination drives, and emergency medical assistance for those who cannot afford private healthcare.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Low-income families
  • Individuals without health insurance
  • Children under 12 years
  • Pregnant women
  • Chronically ill patients

Ready to Apply?

Disability Support Services

Rehabilitation, education, employment opportunities, accessibility improvements, and counseling for people with physical and mental disabilities

"Ability Beyond Disability."

Disability Support Services

We offer comprehensive support for individuals with physical, sensory, and intellectual disabilities, including rehabilitation programs, assistive devices, vocational training, and accessibility improvements to promote independence and inclusion.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Individuals with certified disabilities
  • Age 5 years and above
  • Residents of the country
  • Need for rehabilitation or assistive devices
  • Interest in vocational training

Ready to Apply?

Education Support Services

Scholarships, free schools, after-school programs, literacy drives, and digital learning initiatives for underprivileged children and youth

"Learning Opens Doors to Tomorrow."

Education Support Services

Our education support programs provide scholarships, free schooling, after-school tutoring, and digital learning resources to underprivileged children and youth, ensuring equal access to quality education and opportunities for academic success.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Students aged 5-21 years
  • Family income below threshold
  • Good academic performance
  • Children of single parents
  • Students with learning difficulties

Ready to Apply?

Rehabilitation of Drug Addicts

We assist in rehabilitating individuals addicted to drugs, improving vocational skills and integrating them into society

"Recovery is Possible."

Rehabilitation of Drug Addicts

Our rehabilitation programs provide comprehensive treatment, counseling, vocational training, and social reintegration support for individuals struggling with substance abuse, helping them rebuild their lives and achieve long-term recovery.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Individuals aged 18-60 years
  • Diagnosed substance dependency
  • Willingness to participate in treatment
  • Residents of the country
  • Medical clearance for treatment

Ready to Apply?

Food & Nutrition Programs

Food banks, community kitchens, nutritional awareness drives, and meal distribution to fight hunger and malnutrition

"No One Should Sleep Hungry."

Food & Nutrition Programs

We combat hunger and malnutrition through food distribution programs, community kitchens, nutritional education, and meal supplementation initiatives, ensuring that no family goes to bed hungry and children receive proper nutrition for healthy development.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Families below poverty line
  • Single-parent households
  • Children under 15 years
  • Pregnant and lactating mothers
  • Elderly living alone

Ready to Apply?

Women Empowerment Programs

Support through skill training, legal aid, micro-financing, and protection against domestic violence to help women lead self-reliant lives

"Empowering Women, Empowering Communities."

Women Empowerment Programs

Our women empowerment initiatives provide skill development training, micro-financing opportunities, legal aid, protection against domestic violence, and leadership development programs to help women achieve economic independence and social empowerment.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Women aged 18-60 years
  • Low-income households
  • Interest in skill development
  • Victims of domestic violence
  • Widows or single mothers

Ready to Apply?

Social Welfare Our Mission

Building a Compassionate Society

Social welfare is the collective commitment to protect and uplift vulnerable individuals and communities providing food, shelter, healthcare, education and opportunities so everyone can live with dignity.

Child Care
Protection, education and foster support for children in need.
Elderly Support
Health checks, companionship and safe living options for seniors.
Disability Support
Rehabilitation and accessibility improvements.
Healthcare
Mobile clinics, vaccination drives and medical aid.
Education Support
Scholarships and learning programs for underprivileged youth.
Food Security
Nutrition programs and community feeding initiatives.

Social welfare is about caring for people and communities by providing support, protection, and opportunities. It ensures access to food, shelter, healthcare, and education, while also empowering the vulnerable to live with dignity and hope. Together, we build a fairer, kinder, and stronger society.
